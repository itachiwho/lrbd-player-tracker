Notices & Features

Advanced Tracking

Players are now tracked, so even if they change their FiveM username, they will remain in their assigned shift groups.

Shift Groups Source

Shift groups load from Google Sheet, If that sheet is slow or changes format, roles may briefly show as "-" until it loads.

Refresh & Updates

The list auto-refreshes every 30s. You can also click the refresh icon. "Last updated" time shows the most recent successful fetch.

Search & Filters

Search matches by player Name, ID, and License ID. The Shift filter shows only players in specific shifts.
